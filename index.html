This is the peace Lily plant!

Peace Lily plants are known to be native to hot, moist shady-tropical rainforests! The best care for this plant is attentive watering!

But remember there shouldn't be any standing water around the plant.

This is the Cheese Plant, also called 'Monstera Deliciosa' is a species of flowering plant native to tropical forests of southern Mexico, south to Panama

There are several steps you must take in order to maintain a healthy, sexy cheese plant, this means you must mist the moss on the pole regularly.

You must also position your Monstera in bright light but avoid full sun and the scorching rays of mid-day and water thoroughly at irrigation and let water drain from the holes.

This is the Aloe Vera plant!

Interestingly, the aloe vera is known for its healing properties and is a very useful plant!

You must water this plant only when dried out. In addition to this don't water at all in winter
