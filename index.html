This is the Peace Lily plant!

Peace Lily plants are known to be native to hot, moist shady-tropical rainforests! The best care for this plant is attentive watering!

But remember there shouldn't be any standing water around the plant!

This is the Cheese plant and the Snake plant!

Occassionally water your cheese plant and remember a fertiliser each month or year!

In regards to your snake plant, it doesn't require much water but it should be watered regularly!

Do avoid trimming your snake plants as they do not stimulate growth!

This is the Aloe Vera plant!

Interestingly, the aloe vera is known for its healing properties and is a very useful plant!

You must water this plant only when dried out. In addition to this don't water at all in winter!

Finally ensure that the Aloe Vera plant has a drainage hole!
